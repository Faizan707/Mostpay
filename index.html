Services We Provide

Payment Settlements and Statements

Our company ensures that payments are promptly and securely deposited into customers' accounts. In addition, we offer the ability to generate online statements for each transaction record.

Payments Traceability

The state-of-the-art built-in tool enables to monitor the processing of payments throughout the entire processing procedure.

Compliance Support

One of the primary role of our compliance team is to assist merchants in adhering to the legal requirements and regulations that are applicable to their respective locations and industries.

Support of Extensive Payment Methods

We accept and provide a large selection of payment methods. The most popular ones—debit/credit cards, bank transfers (B2B), and digital wallets like Apple Pay, Google Pay, Paypal, Revolut, Payoneer, Alipay, etc.—are the ones we mostly concentrate on.

Personal Account Manager

We are dedicated to provide first-rate services, and as part of that commitment, we provide a unique personal account manager to each and every merchant.

Cutting Edge Banking Services
